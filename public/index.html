basic usage:

Enter a short summary of the email on the left and hit generate.

That's it! Wait for a couple seconds and you should be able to copy your email by hitting copy on the right.

tips:

  • include keywords in your summary, i.e: formal, informal, short, long etc.
  • include a sender and a recipient so that the salutations match
